Ok, you're installed, and you think you're ready to start? Well we beg to differ! Before you can throw yourself into a battle, you need to learn how to use your Launcher! The following steps will show you how to set up your custom character!



STEP 1: Choosing a player When you look at the launcher for the first time, you'll need to pick a player from your JK Dir so that you can make a TDIR character. So hit that [Select Player] button, Goto your JK Dir, then the Player folder, and pick the one with your name! Hit OK! And you're set!

 

STEP 2: Choose your fighter Once you've picked a player, you can choose a character. Yes, that big long list on the left is all the characters in the game. Find the one you'd like to be, then highlight it by clicking.

 

STEP 3: Choose your attacks! Now that you've got your character, lets choose some attacks! Push the [Customize Char] button, and have a peek at what pops up! These menus allow you to pick your attacks, their colors and your aura colors. Ball is your Spirit Bomb attack, Beam and strong beam are your other attacks.

 

STEP 4: Start playing! Once you've chosen attacks, hit the [Save Char/Customs] button.  Make sure the TDiR: Enabled/Disabled button says “TDiR: Enabled” and then you're ready to start! Please note that you should look through the rest of the manual before playing so that you don't miss anything important!
